Salmon Bake

Description

Sushi Bake is basically a California roll in casserole form. Layers of rice, seasonings, and fillings get broiled in the oven
to make a warm, creamy dish with amazing flavor. The best part about this recipe is that itâ€™s super easy to make and even easier to eat! It tastes better than store-bought sushi rolls because you can customize your own sushi bake with whatever ingredients you have on hand. You can even make it vegetarian by replacing the fish with tofu or vegetables such as carrots or zucchini slices.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sushi rice Calrose rice works as well
  • 3 tbs rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs sugar

Steps

  1. Cook Rice according to package directions. While rice is cooking, finely shred the imitation crab and add that to a bowl with sriracha, wasabi, green onion, cream cheese, and kewpie mayo. Stir to combine.
  2. Add rice vinegar, salt & sugar. Stir to combine
  3. Press the rice into the bottom of an 8x8 baking dish. Sprinkle with furikake seasoning.
  4. Broil for 10 minutes.
