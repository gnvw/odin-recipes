Fruit Tart

Description

Loaded with a lush assortment of seasonal berries and other fruit, there’s nothing quite as lovely as a Fresh Fruit Tart. You’ll also learn some baking basics, like a tart crust and pastry cream, along the way.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sushi rice Calrose rice works as well
  • 3 tbs rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs sugar

Steps

  1. 1. Cook Rice according to package directions. While rice is cooking, finely shred the imitation crab and add that to a bowl with sriracha, wasabi, green onion, cream cheese, and kewpie mayo. Stir to combine.
  2. 2. Add rice vinegar, salt & sugar. Stir to combine
  3. 3. Press the rice into the bottom of an 8x8 baking dish. Sprinkle with furikake seasoning.
  4. 4. Broil for 10 minutes.
